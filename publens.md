# PubLens

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

PubLens ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our macOS application handles information.

---

## 🔒 Data Collection

PubLens does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

- We do not require sign-in
- We do not track users
- We do not collect analytics
- We do not use cookies or third-party trackers

---

## 🌐 Network Usage

PubLens makes network requests only to:

- **https://pub.dev**

These requests are used solely to fetch publicly available package information (such as package versions).

No personal data is sent with these requests.

---

## 💾 Data Storage

PubLens does **not store any user data** locally or remotely.

All searches are temporary and exist only during app usage.

---

## 📋 Clipboard Access

PubLens allows users to copy package versions to the clipboard.

- Clipboard access is only triggered by user action
- No clipboard data is stored or transmitted

---

## 🔐 Third-Party Services

PubLens does not use any third-party analytics, ads, or tracking services.

---

## 👶 Children's Privacy

PubLens does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top.
