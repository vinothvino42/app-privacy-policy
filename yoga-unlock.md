# Privacy Policy for Yoga Unlock

**Last Updated: June 23, 2026**

## Privacy Policy

Welcome to Yoga Unlock. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Yoga Unlock collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

Yoga Unlock is designed to help users reduce distractions by requiring a short yoga session before accessing selected apps.

The app may collect and store the following information locally on your device:

* App blocking and unlocking preferences.
* Selected apps and categories configured through Apple's Screen Time APIs.
* Yoga session settings, including selected poses, pose duration, rest duration, and unlock duration.
* Notification preferences.
* Progress-related settings, such as completed yoga sessions and streak information (if applicable).

Yoga Unlock does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Location data
* Contacts
* Photos
* Messages
* Browsing history

## Screen Time Permissions

Yoga Unlock uses Apple's Screen Time APIs, including the Family Controls and Managed Settings frameworks, to block and temporarily unblock apps selected by the user.

Information accessed through these permissions remains on your device and is used solely to provide the app's functionality.

## Notifications

Yoga Unlock may request permission to send notifications. Notifications are used only to:

* Remind users when blocked apps require a yoga session to unlock.
* Inform users when an unlock session begins or expires.
* Provide optional reminders related to app-blocking schedules and yoga sessions.

Notification data is not shared with third parties.

## Data Storage

Yoga Unlock stores app settings and preferences locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms, including UserDefaults and App Group storage when required for app functionality.

We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data.

## Third-Party Services

Yoga Unlock does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

The app may rely on Apple-provided services and frameworks necessary for functionality, which are governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Yoga Unlock does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected, the app presents minimal privacy risks to children.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

Because Yoga Unlock does not collect personal information or maintain user accounts, there is generally no personal data stored by us to access, modify, or delete.

You can remove all app data at any time by:

* Deleting the app.
* Resetting the app's settings from within the app (if available).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
