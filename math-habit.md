# Privacy Policy for Math Habit

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

Math Habit ("the app") is a math practice application designed to help users improve their arithmetic skills. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

Math Habit does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app does not require you to create an account, and no personal data such as name, email, or contact information is collected.

The app may store your quiz progress, settings, and scores **locally on your device** to provide a personalized experience. This data is not transmitted to any server or third party.

## Analytics and Third-Party Services

Math Habit does **not** use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services. All app functionality is self-contained and does not communicate with external servers.

## Children’s Privacy

Math Habit is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since no personal information is collected, the app complies with privacy regulations for children, such as COPPA.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
